Our Story

Peter Skotte is a San Diego-based technologist with a background in business (SDSU, BS in Business Administration). He built his career in data engineering, business intelligence, and automation — working with companies ranging from startups to national brands.

Skotte Analytics was founded on a simple idea: most businesses are drowning in manual processes they don't need to be doing. We write code, build tools, and deploy AI that actually works — not PowerPoint presentations about what might be possible someday.
