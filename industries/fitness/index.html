Fitness & Gyms

AI & Automation for Fitness Centers & Gyms

The average gym loses 50% of new members within the first 6 months, with most cancellations happening silently through payment failures rather than active decisions.

The Challenge

What's holding fitness & gyms businesses back

Gyms and fitness centers face a unique business model challenge: they sell memberships that members actively try to cancel. Retention is everything, and it depends on engagement — members who show up stay. Automation keeps members engaged through their critical first months, catches payment failures before they become cancellations, and gives operators the data to optimize their offerings.

Common Pain Points

  • Member retention drops off a cliff after the first 90 days when the initial motivation fades
  • Personal training revenue is limited by trainer availability and scheduling friction
  • Membership billing failures from expired cards create silent churn that goes unnoticed for weeks
  • Class scheduling is reactive — popular times are overcrowded while off-peak classes are empty
  • Lead follow-up from website inquiries and walk-ins is inconsistent and poorly tracked
